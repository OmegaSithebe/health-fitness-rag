# Dietary Guidelines and Recommendations

## Macronutrient Ratios

### General Health
- Protein: 10-35% of calories
- Carbohydrates: 45-65% of calories
- Fats: 20-35% of calories

### For Muscle Building
- Protein: 25-35% of calories (1.6-2.2g per kg bodyweight)
- Carbohydrates: 40-50% of calories
- Fats: 20-30% of calories

### For Weight Loss
- Protein: 25-35% of calories
- Carbohydrates: 30-40% of calories
- Fats: 20-30% of calories

## Meal Timing Considerations

### Pre-Workout (1-2 hours before)
- Light meal with carbs and protein
- Examples: Banana, oatmeal, protein shake
- Avoid heavy fats

### Post-Workout (within 30-60 minutes)
- Protein and carbs for recovery
- Examples: Protein shake with fruit, chocolate milk
- Aim for 20-40g protein

### General Guidelines
- Eat every 3-4 hours
- Don't skip meals
- Listen to hunger cues
- Plan meals in advance

## Foods to Focus On
- Lean proteins (chicken, fish, lean beef)
- Whole grains (oats, brown rice, quinoa)
- Fruits and vegetables (variety of colors)
- Healthy fats (avocado, nuts, olive oil)
- Probiotics (yogurt, kefir, fermented foods)

## Foods to Limit
- Processed foods
- Added sugars
- Trans fats
- Excessive alcohol
- Highly refined carbohydrates
