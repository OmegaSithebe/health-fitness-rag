# Nutrition and Meal Planning Guide

## Balanced Meal Formula
- **Protein** (palm-sized portion): Chicken, fish, eggs, tofu, legumes
- **Carbohydrates** (fist-sized portion): Quinoa, brown rice, sweet potato, oats
- **Vegetables** (2 fist-sized portions): Broccoli, spinach, bell peppers, kale
- **Healthy Fats** (thumb-sized portion): Avocado, nuts, olive oil

## Sample Daily Meal Plan

### Breakfast (7:00 AM)
- Oatmeal with berries and almond butter
- 2 scrambled eggs
- Green tea or black coffee

### Morning Snack (10:00 AM)
- Greek yogurt with honey
- Handful of almonds
- Apple

### Lunch (1:00 PM)
- Grilled chicken breast (4-6 oz)
- Quinoa (1 cup cooked)
- Roasted vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers)
- Olive oil drizzle

### Afternoon Snack (4:00 PM)
- Rice cakes with avocado
- Protein shake (optional)

### Dinner (7:00 PM)
- Salmon fillet (4-6 oz)
- Sweet potato (medium)
- Steamed asparagus
- Mixed green salad

## Hydration Guide
- Drink at least 2-3 liters of water daily
- More if you're active or in hot weather
- Consider electrolyte drinks during intense workouts

## Supplements (Consult Doctor First)
- Protein powder (whey or plant-based)
- Creatine (for strength training)
- Omega-3 fish oil
- Vitamin D
- Magnesium
