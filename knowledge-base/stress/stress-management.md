# Stress Management Techniques

## Understanding Stress
- Acute stress: Short-term response to challenges
- Chronic stress: Long-term, harmful to health
- Eustress: Positive stress that motivates

## Physical Techniques

### Deep Breathing
- 4-7-8 Technique:
  - Breathe in for 4 seconds
  - Hold for 7 seconds
  - Exhale for 8 seconds
- Repeat 3-4 times

### Progressive Muscle Relaxation
1. Tense muscles for 5-10 seconds
2. Release tension for 10-15 seconds
3. Progress through body:
   - Feet → Legs → Abdomen → Chest
   - Arms → Shoulders → Neck → Face

### Physical Activity
- Cardiovascular exercise (releases endorphins)
- Yoga (combines movement and breathing)
- Walking outdoors
- Any form of movement (20-30 minutes)

## Mental Techniques

### Meditation
- Start with 5 minutes daily
- Use guided meditations
- Focus on breath
- Practice mindfulness

### Cognitive Restructuring
- Identify negative thoughts
- Challenge their validity
- Replace with realistic thoughts
- Practice self-compassion

### Journaling
- Write about stressful events
- Identify patterns
- Express gratitude
- List accomplishments

## Lifestyle Modifications

### Daily Practices
- Maintain consistent sleep schedule
- Eat balanced meals regularly
- Stay hydrated
- Exercise 3-5 times weekly
- Schedule relaxation time

### Setting Boundaries
- Learn to say "no"
- Delegate tasks when possible
- Set realistic expectations
- Take breaks when needed

### Social Support
- Connect with friends and family
- Join support groups
- Share experiences
- Ask for help when needed

## Stress and Exercise Connection
- Exercise is one of the best stress relievers
- Cardiovascular exercise reduces cortisol
- Strength training builds resilience
- Consistency matters more than intensity
- Find activities you enjoy

## When to Seek Professional Help
- If stress significantly impacts daily function
- If you experience anxiety or depression symptoms
- If physical symptoms are severe
- If you've experienced trauma
