# General Health and Wellness Guide

## Foundational Health Principles

### Physical Health
1. Regular exercise (150+ minutes moderate activity weekly)
2. Balanced nutrition (variety of whole foods)
3. Adequate hydration (2-3 liters daily)
4. Quality sleep (7-9 hours)
5. Preventive healthcare (regular check-ups)

### Mental Health
1. Stress management techniques
2. Social connections
3. Purpose and meaning
4. Mental stimulation
5. Rest and recovery

### Daily Habits for Health

#### Morning Routine
- Wake up at consistent time
- Hydrate immediately
- Movement (stretch, walk, or exercise)
- Healthy breakfast
- Plan the day

#### Throughout the Day
- Take movement breaks hourly
- Practice good posture
- Stay hydrated
- Eat balanced meals
- Practice mindfulness

#### Evening Routine
- Wind down 1-2 hours before bed
- Reduce screen time
- Prepare for next day
- Reflect on positives
- Sleep at consistent time

## Common Health Concerns

### Sedentary Lifestyle Issues
- Risk: Weight gain, muscle loss, poor circulation
- Solution: Movement breaks, desk stretches, daily activity

### Poor Diet Effects
- Risk: Low energy, nutritional deficiencies, weight issues
- Solution: Balanced meals, meal planning, whole foods

### Sleep Deprivation
- Risk: Poor recovery, mood issues, decreased performance
- Solution: Sleep hygiene, consistent schedule, proper environment

## Health Metrics to Monitor
- Weight (not obsessively, trend matters)
- Energy levels
- Mood and mental state
- Physical performance
- Sleep quality
- Recovery from exercise

## When to See Healthcare Professional
- Persistent pain
- Unexplained symptoms
- Significant weight changes
- Mood or mental health concerns
- Before starting intensive exercise program
- Injuries that don't improve
