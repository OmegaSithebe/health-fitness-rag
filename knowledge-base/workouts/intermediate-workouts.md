# Intermediate Workout Routines

## Push/Pull/Legs Split

### Push Day (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)
1. **Bench Press (dumbbell or barbell)** - 4 sets of 8-10 reps
2. **Overhead Press** - 4 sets of 8-10 reps
3. **Incline Dumbbell Press** - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
4. **Lateral Raises** - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
5. **Triceps Pushdowns** - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
6. **Triceps Dips** - 3 sets of 10-12 reps

### Pull Day (Back, Biceps, Rear Delts)
1. **Pull-ups or Lat Pulldowns** - 4 sets of 8-10 reps
2. **Dumbbell Rows** - 4 sets of 10-12 reps
3. **Seated Cable Rows** - 3 sets of 12 reps
4. **Face Pulls** - 3 sets of 15 reps
5. **Barbell Curls** - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
6. **Hammer Curls** - 3 sets of 12-15 reps

### Leg Day (Quads, Hamstrings, Glutes)
1. **Squats** - 4 sets of 8-10 reps
2. **Romanian Deadlifts** - 4 sets of 10-12 reps
3. **Leg Press** - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
4. **Leg Curls** - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
5. **Calf Raises** - 4 sets of 15-20 reps

### Progression Tips
- Increase weight by 2.5-5 lbs when you can complete all reps
- Focus on form before adding weight
- Aim for 2-3 minutes rest between sets for compound exercises
