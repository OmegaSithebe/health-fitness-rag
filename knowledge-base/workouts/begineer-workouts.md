# Beginner Workout Routines

## Full Body Workout (3x per week)

### Warm-up (5-10 minutes)
- Light jogging or jumping jacks
- Arm circles
- Leg swings
- Torso twists

### Main Workout

#### Day 1
1. **Bodyweight Squats** - 3 sets of 12 reps
   - Stand with feet shoulder-width apart
   - Lower hips back and down as if sitting in chair
   - Keep chest up and weight on heels
   
2. **Push-ups (modified)** - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
   - Start on hands and knees
   - Lower chest toward ground
   - Push back up

3. **Bent-over Rows (with bands or light weights)** - 3 sets of 12 reps
   - Hinge at hips, keep back flat
   - Pull weights toward chest
   - Squeeze shoulder blades together

4. **Plank** - 3 sets of 20-30 seconds
   - Hold push-up position
   - Keep body straight
   - Engage core

5. **Glute Bridges** - 3 sets of 15 reps
   - Lie on back, knees bent
   - Lift hips toward ceiling
   - Squeeze glutes at top

### Cool-down (5-10 minutes)
- Standing quad stretch
- Chest stretch
- Hamstring stretch
- Child's pose

## Rest and Recovery
- Rest 48-72 hours between full body sessions
- Listen to your body
- Stay hydrated
- Get adequate sleep (7-9 hours)
