# Sleep Optimization Guide

## Why Sleep Matters
- Muscle recovery and growth
- Hormone regulation
- Mental health and cognitive function
- Immune system support
- Performance enhancement

## Recommended Sleep Duration
- Adults (18-64): 7-9 hours
- Older adults (65+): 7-8 hours
- Athletes: 8-10 hours

## Creating an Ideal Sleep Environment

### Temperature
- Optimal: 60-67°F (15-19°C)
- Cooler temperatures signal sleep onset

### Lighting
- Total darkness or use eye mask
- Reduce blue light exposure 1-2 hours before bed
- Use blue light filters on devices

### Noise
- Quiet environment preferred
- Consider white noise or earplugs

### Bedroom
- Use bed only for sleep and intimacy
- Keep room clutter-free
- Use comfortable pillows and mattress

## Sleep Hygiene Practices

### Before Bed (2 hours before)
1. Reduce caffeine intake (stop after 2 PM)
2. Limit alcohol consumption
3. Avoid large meals
4. Stop working/studying
5. Dim lighting in home

### Bedtime Routine (30 minutes before)
1. Warm shower or bath (1-2°F drop in body temperature)
2. Read a book (not on a screen)
3. Light stretching or gentle yoga
4. Meditation or deep breathing
5. Journal about tomorrow's tasks

### Morning Routine
1. Wake up at the same time daily
2. Get sunlight exposure immediately
3. Morning movement (walk, stretch)
4. Consistent breakfast time

## Common Sleep Issues

### Insomnia
- Difficulty falling asleep
- Difficulty staying asleep
- See healthcare professional if persistent

### Sleep Apnea
- Loud snoring
- Gasping during sleep
- Daytime fatigue
- Consult doctor for treatment

## Sleep and Fitness Connection
- Sleep is when muscle repair happens
- Poor sleep reduces performance
- Adequate sleep improves gym performance
- Sleep influences metabolism and weight
